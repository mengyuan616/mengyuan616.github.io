About me

I am a multimedia journalist currently based in the Bay Area. I am also pursuing my master’s degree in journalism at the University of California, Berkeley.

I love to write, code and film.

I’ve been writing-focused my whole college life, but I’m also equipped with basic coding and data analysis skills that support my passion for reporting data-driven stories.

I’ve had media experience in both China and the U.S., including interning at Caixin Media and being a student reporter at The Daily Texan.

My reporting interests include climate, recycling policies, public health and social justice.
